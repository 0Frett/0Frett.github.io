About

I'm currently a second-year M.S. student in Computer Science at NTU, advised by Prof. Shou-De Lin. My research focuses on Natural Language Processing (NLP), particularly in the following areas:

  • LLM Uncertainty/Confidence Estimation: How can we quantify the uncertainty of LLMs for a given question and assess the confidence of their responses across different tasks?.
  • LLM Evaluation: Developing automatic and contamination-controlled benchmarks to systematically evaluate diverse abilities of LLMs.
  • LLM Applications: Leveraging LLMs to address domain-specific challenges.

I expect to graduate in June 2026 and am applying for Ph.D. programs starting in Fall 2026.
